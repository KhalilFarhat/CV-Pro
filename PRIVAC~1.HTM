Privacy Policy

CVpro takes the privacy of its users very seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and with whom we share it. This policy also outlines the measures we take to protect the security of this information.

Information Collection

CVpro collects information from its users in a number of ways, including:

  • Information provided by users when creating an account or updating their profile.
  • Information provided by users when using the CVpro service, such as uploading their CV, searching for job opportunities, or applying for jobs.
  • Information provided by users through feedback, surveys, or other forms of communication.
  • Technical information, such as IP address, browser type, and device type, may also be collected automatically when users use the CVpro service.


Information Use

CVpro uses the information we collect to provide and improve our services, to communicate with users, and for administrative purposes. This includes:

  • Providing users with access to the CVpro service and their personal information, such as their CV and job applications.
  • Matching users with potential job opportunities based on their CV and job preferences.
  • Sending users notifications about new job opportunities, updates to their job applications, or other relevant information.
  • Improving the CVpro service, such as fixing bugs, adding new features, or improving performance.


Information Sharing

CVpro will not share users' personal information with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • With the user's consent, such as when they apply for a job through the CVpro service.
  • As required by law, such as in response to a subpoena or court order.
  • To protect the security of our users and the CVpro service, such as in the case of suspected fraud or abuse.


Information Security

CVpro takes appropriate measures to protect the security of its users' information, including:

  • Storing information securely in our database.
  • Using encryption to protect sensitive information, such as passwords and payment information.
  • Regularly monitoring our systems for potential security vulnerabilities and taking steps to address any issues that are identified.


Changes to this Policy

CVpro may make changes to this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any changes, we will notify users
